Effects of Pollution on People

The nonstop release of chemicals and pollutants into the atmosphere has deadly effects on our health:

Every year, more people are killed by pollutants from toxic air to contaminated water than by all war and violence. Pollution is also responsible for more deaths than AIDS, tuberculosis, and malaria combined.

Pollution & our Health

  • The world health organization has declared that the air we breathe is laced with cancer-causing substances
  • A polluted beach causes rashes, hepatitis, gastroenteritis, diarrhea, encephalitis, stomach aches and vomiting
  • Air pollution has contributed to over a million deaths in China
  • Water pollution contamines food sources
